Welcome to GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter of PCCoE
PCCOE (GeeksforGeeks Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering) student chapter is a community of students in Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering, Pune, India who are interested in programming, technology, and computer science. The chapter is affiliated with GeeksforGeeks, a popular platform for computer science enthusiasts.
The primary objective of GFG PCCOE student chapter is to create a supportive and collaborative environment for students to learn, grow, and excel in the field of computer science. They organize various events, workshops, seminars, and competitions throughout the year to facilitate learning and skill development. The chapter provides a platform for students to interact with experts in the field, share knowledge and ideas, and build networks with fellow enthusiasts.
